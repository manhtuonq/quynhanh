INTRODUCTION

Name: Nguyen Quynh Anh

My name is Nguyen Quynh Anh, a student from Thang Long High School, and I am passionate about creativity, performing, and community engagement. Through my involvement in school leadership and event organization, I developed strong collaboration and communication skills, which helped me realize that while creativity drives my motivation, I aspire to grow beyond instinct and emotion toward structured knowledge in core profit and strategy management. This was also when my interest in business and management began, as I became increasingly fascinated by how ideas can be turned into effective plans, organized into sustainable systems, and scaled to create meaningful social impact. This is my portfolio, my gallery where I proudly tell you the story of my meaningful journey.
