About the Competition

Overview

This online competition is organized annually by the Central Committee of the Youth Union and the Ministry of Education and Training, open to all Vietnamese students and young people. It uses an online multiple-choice format to spread President Ho Chi Minh's ideology, conducted across three main rounds.

My Achievement

Through serious, proactive, and persistent effort in studying the ideology, ethics, and style of President Ho Chi Minh, I achieved Second Prize in the competition. This accomplishment not only recognizes my academic commitment and self-discipline, but also serves as a strong motivation for me to continue developing a sense of social responsibility and contributing positively to the community and society.
